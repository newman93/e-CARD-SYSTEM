Add new category
  1. Log in to your admin account.
  2. Check if category you want to add do not exist.
  3. Click "Add New Category".
  4. Write the name of the category and choose the image of the category.
  5. Clik "Submit" button.
Add new media
  1. Log in to your admin account.
  2. Check if media you want to add do not exist.
  3. Click "Add New Media".
  4. Write the name of the category and media.
  5. Choose the image to upload.
  6. Clik "Submit" button.
Send e-Card
  1. Choose the category.
  2. Choose the media.
  3. Write your name, your e-mail address, your firend address and eMessage.
  4. Clik "Send" button.
